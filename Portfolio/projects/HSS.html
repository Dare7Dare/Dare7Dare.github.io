Human Support System

is a mobile application which allows users to schedule regular or emergency medical appointments for themselves or their relatives, schedule a one on one session with a specialist, track their therapy progress, get reminders, pay for the services and get tests results.

I worked on the front-end development of the PSS mobile application using mostly the Flutter framework and JavaScript. I was heavily involved in creating the looks and functionalities of this application.
